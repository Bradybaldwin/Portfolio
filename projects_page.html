Selected Work
Educational VR Experience
I worked with a team of four students to demonstrate the way that machine vision can experience sight line obstruction from objects superseding others in the field of view. We used a combination of coding and animation to complete this process, working in Autodesk Maya to model all of the assets in the project and assembling them in Unity.
CARVE Lab Model
This lab model was needed for an upcoming evacuation simulation study. It was created to scale, with many minor details mirroring the actual lab as the results from VR trials were to be compared to results from real world trials. The study is currently ongoing and uses the lab model continually, and other studies plan on using the model in the future.
User Research for Local Theater
Term-long research project conducted for a course at CMU with a small team. Focused on increasing donations for the Kelly-Strayhorn Theater in Pittsburgh. We worked to identify user needs through a variety of methods and prortyped an innovative digital membership solution for the theater.
