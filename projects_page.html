Selected Work
Pandemic Professors Capstone Project
I am currently working with a team of MHCI students to improve the tutor-student pairing process at Pandemic Professors, a non-profit organization aimed at providing tutoring services for low-income families negatively affected by the online learning environment. I am the lead researcher on our team, using a variety of methods to diagnose pain points in the current system and work towards prototyping solutions.
Fake News Detection Game
I worked in a group with 5 other MCHI students to develop a prototype for a social detection game where players must use critical thinking skills to identify real and fake news articles. We determined what skills to build and what aspects of the game to develop based on user testing, expert interviews and literature review.
User Research for Local Theater
Term-long research project conducted for a course at CMU with a small team. Focused on increasing donations for the Kelly-Strayhorn Theater in Pittsburgh. We worked to identify user needs through a variety of methods and prortyped an innovative digital membership solution for the theater.
Educational VR Experience
I worked with a team of four students to demonstrate the way that machine vision can experience sight line obstruction from objects superseding others in the field of view. We used a combination of coding and animation to complete this process, working in Autodesk Maya to model all of the assets in the project and assembling them in Unity.
